about us

WE ARE INDEtrans

Founded in 2013, INDtrans is an associate company of Tak Machinery & Leasing Ltd. which is a Public Limited Company listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE).

INDtrans thus was started with a core team comprising of business leaders who own and run the company. INDtrans prides itself by having currently an active network in more than 65 countries.

INDtrans has focused on hiring highly competent professionals from the logistics industry and provided them with a working environment in which they can fully realize their potential and deliver performance which guarantees your satisfaction and our success.

We value every customer and understand that each project requires a unique solution. This unique combination of an individual approach within an international framework ensures that we deliver top-quality customized logistics solutions to our clientele every time.

It is our constant endeavor to develop better and more efficient ways to do business by implementing the latest technology and increasing the number of services we provide.

Our mission

We have committed ourselves to being the leading global logistics provider in terms of Quality Service, Constant Innovation not forgetting our Social Responsibility.

We recognize that people are the foundation of our success and make every effort to provide them with a fulfilling and engaging working environment.

We carry out our commitment to customers by being responsive to their needs and providing them with logistics solutions that support their success.
