AIR FREIGHT

Around the clock, Around the world
Speed, reliability and cost-effectiveness are the main attributes of a successful air cargo supplier.

Our extensive global network allows us to deliver reliable air freight services designed to your individual needs.

Standardized products, door-to-door deliveries, heavy loads, transportation of hazardous goods, charter of less-than-container and full-container loads as well as special solutions for automotive, fashion and food are all part of our product portfolio.

With our select carriers, we ensure that your shipments arrive not only safely but also on schedule.

Our IT system offers real-time status information along the entire supply chain: from pick-up to the delivery - from your desk to your customer’s.

Whether you are shipping domestically or across international borders, our experts will work closely with you in implementing a solution that is designed to suit your needs.
